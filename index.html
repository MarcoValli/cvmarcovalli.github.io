Projects

  • Titel: MDMA

  • Course: Workshop Tirocinante
  • Description: A map that outlines a one-day journey through Milan’s biggest urban park: Milano Parco Nord. The projects is called “MDMA” since it wants to create an interactive experience based on the following activities: Meditiation, Music and Arts.
  • Title: Jacques-André Boiffard

  • Course: Storia della Comunicazione Visiva
  • Description: This editorial project analyzes Jacques-André Boiffard’s life and works. Boiffard was a French photographer whose style was very peculiar, in fact he was also a doctor and many of his photographs remind radiographs.
  • Title: London Calling

  • Course: Fondamenti del Progetto
  • Description: What if an important aged record was released nowadays? Me and my team worked on this idea by dealing with the record “London Calling” by The Clash (1979).
  • Title: NASA

  • Course: Elementi Visivi
  • Description: This project deals with the difficulties of creating a space based on different layers. Every layer is represented by an area limited by a hexagonal structure that reminds the one seen in 2001: A Space Odyssey. This model is conceived to be a recreational space for NASA.
